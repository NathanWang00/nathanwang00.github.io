Take as Needed

Design & Programming
Production Details
  • Genre: Story-driven pharmaceutical puzzler
  • Development Time: 6 months
  • Team size: 10 (1 Director/Composer, 2 Writers, 4 Designers/Programmers, 3 Artists)
  • Engine and Language: Unity, C#
Overview

Take as Needed is a near future puzzle and visual novel game. The player works as a scientist at a pharmaceutical company whose actions can influence the world around them. The game also explores themes of addiction, mental health, and stress. Additionally, the project was funded by a research grant where we looked into innovative and more appropriate ways into portraying mental health in video games.

In this project, I was a designer and programmer. My main role was developing the puzzle/drug mechanics. For example, creating the price and volatility system, designing the chemical connections, and programming the controls.
